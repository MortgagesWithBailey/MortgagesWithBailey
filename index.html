Mortgage Market Update

The latest news and trends from Canadian Mortgage Trends

May 19, 2026

Ontario & Canada Mortgage News

  • Inflation Rises Slightly: Canadian inflation rose to 2.8% in April, largely driven by energy prices, though core measures of inflation cooled. This adds slight uncertainty to the Bank of Canada's rate path.
  • Spring Market Thaw: The Canadian Real Estate Association (CREA) reported that April home sales fell compared with a year ago, as buyers remain hesitant despite the busy spring season.
  • Construction Push: The federal government is sticking to its pledge to double the pace of home building across the country, even as the construction industry faces economic headwinds.
  • Variable Rate Popularity: The CMHC notes that borrowers are increasingly turning to variable-rate and shorter-term mortgages as renewal pressures begin to ease.
Source: Canadian Mortgage Trends
