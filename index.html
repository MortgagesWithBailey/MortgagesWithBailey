Mortgage Market Update

The latest news and trends from Canadian Mortgage Trends

June 24, 2026

Ontario & Canada Mortgage News

  • Inflation Spikes: A recent spike in gasoline prices has pushed Canadian inflation to its highest level since 2023, complicating the Bank of Canada's rate decisions.
  • OSFI Cuts Capital Buffers: The banking regulator, OSFI, is cutting the bank capital buffer requirement to encourage institutions to "take risk" and boost lending across the economy.
  • Mortgage Pressure: A new report from Mortgage Professionals Canada (MPC) indicates that many current mortgage holders have very little financial room to absorb higher payments at renewal.
  • Lending Outlook: Following OSFI's decision, BoC Governor Tiff Macklem warned that a bank capital rule change alone won't be enough to spark immediate lending.
Source: Canadian Mortgage Trends
